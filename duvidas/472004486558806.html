"1: Make dry acetone:

Buy some epsom salts. Bake them in the oven until they're dry (200 degrees for 45 mins to an hour. Grind them up into dust with pestle and mortar.

Take a litre of lab-grade acetone, put a handful of salts in the top, shake for 2 minutes and leave to settle for 24 hours. You now have ""dry acetone""; acetone without any water in it. Keep the cap on unless you're using it or it will pull moisture in from the air and become capable of dissolving cocaine.

2: Dry your drugs out

Chop or grind your coke or mdma up to dust. put a few drips of water, less than a teaspoon, on a plate and zap it in the microwave until it's boiling. Wipe off the water from the hot plate and watch the residue evaporate. Put the drugs on the plate and work it around until it changes constitution, it'll hopefully go chalkier and crunchier. If it turns to mush you've got really, really shitty coke and is unlikely to be more than 10%.

3: Wash your drugs

Put your dry coke in a beaker. Drip acetone over the top until it just covers it, a teaspoon per gram is a good rule of thumb. Stir it up for a few minutes then strain it through a coffee filter. Take the filter and dry it out on a radiator, with a hair dryer or whatever.

If your gear was good or cut with something difficult to wash out you'll lose 10%. If it's street shit you'll lose 90%."
